# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a friendly, inclusive, and respectful community.

## Our standards

- Be kind and considerate.
- Assume positive intent.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Focus on constructive feedback.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hostility.
- Personal attacks or trolling.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may take action to address violations.
